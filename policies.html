1. Parking Liability
Your responsibilities when using our facility
Important: ParkMate provides space rental only. We are not responsible for theft, vandalism, fire, flooding, or any damage to vehicles or their contents. Users park entirely at their own risk.
1
Vehicle Security
Always lock your vehicle. ParkMate security guards conduct regular patrols but cannot guarantee against all incidents. CCTV is operational in Zones A and B.
2
Valuables
Do not leave valuables in visible areas of your vehicle. ParkMate disclaims all liability for items left inside parked vehicles.
3
Structural Damage Claims
Any damage believed to be caused by facility infrastructure must be reported to the security desk within 30 minutes and documented with photos.
2. Code of Conduct
Behaviour expected of all users inside the facility
1
Speed Limit: 10 km/h
All vehicles must travel at or below 10 km/h inside the facility. Speed camera alerts are active in ramp areas.
2
Assigned Slots Only
You must park in the specific slot number assigned to your Token. Parking in any other slot constitutes an infringement.
3
No Overnight Parking
Vehicles left overnight without a Monthly Pass will be flagged after midnight and may be towed at owner's expense.
4
No Hazardous Vehicles
Vehicles carrying flammable cargo, LPG, or any hazardous material are strictly prohibited in enclosed zones.
5
Accessibility Respect
Blue-marked accessible slots are reserved exclusively for badge holders. Violation results in immediate tow.

3. Payments & Wallet
How billing and wallet transactions work
Free Period: The first 15 minutes of every parking session are completely free. No deduction is made if you exit within 15 minutes.
1
Digital Wallet Only
All charges are deducted from your ParkMate Wallet on exit. Cash payments are not accepted at exit gates.
2
Insufficient Balance
If your wallet balance is insufficient at exit, you will be directed to a top-up kiosk. A ₹50 administrative fee applies for assisted exits.
3
Refund Policy
Cancellations made within 5 minutes of booking are fully refunded. After 5 minutes, a ₹20 processing fee applies. No refund after vehicle entry.
4
Wallet Top-Up
You can add funds via UPI, Net Banking, or Debit/Credit card. Top-ups are instant. Minimum top-up: ₹50.
4. Token & Gate Pass Rules
Usage and validity of your digital ticket
1
Single Use Only
Each token is valid for one entry-exit cycle. Re-entry on the same token is not permitted.
2
Non-Transferable
Tokens are tied to a specific vehicle registration. Using a token for a different vehicle voids the booking.
3
QR & Manual Entry
If QR scanning fails, manually enter the 10-character Token ID at the gate keypad. All gates have keypad fallback.
4
Token Expiry
Tokens expire 30 minutes after booking if the vehicle has not entered the facility. Expired tokens are non-refundable.
