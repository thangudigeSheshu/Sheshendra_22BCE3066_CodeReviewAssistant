Origin Story
It started with a 45-minute search for parking.

In 2016, our founder Vikram Anand circled a Chennai mall for 45 minutes looking for a parking spot. When he finally parked — three floors up, 400m from the entrance — he thought: "There has to be a smarter way."

He sketched the first version of ParkMate on a napkin that same evening. The idea was simple: use real-time data to show drivers exactly where to park before they even enter.

Eight years later, ParkMate Prime serves 2.4 million monthly users across 28 Indian cities. The mission hasn't changed: make parking invisible.
